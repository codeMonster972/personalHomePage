Hobbies

I started bringing my oldest to taekwondo as a cheaper alternative to physical therapy. My second and third children also joined and I found myself in the back of the class trying to finish up work on a laptop which didn't work very well. So, I joined as well looking for exercise and the shared experience with my kids.

Fishing with my dad has been an important activity since I was a boy. He would take me back to his childhood home on the coast to catch red fish and speckled trout.

Camping both with a scouting group and in an RV is another way I spend time with the people I care about. I grew up in a group called Royal Rangers, but the best group I've found for my sons is a Trail Life group.
